Pure Polyester Series
Engineered for
the Exterior
10 Year Warranty 20 Year Warranty

Polybond's Pure Polyester range is engineered for architectural exteriors. Formulated with specially selected polyester resins, it delivers outstanding UV resistance, superior colour and gloss retention, and excellent resistance to weathering, humidity, and corrosion.

Whether exposed to the coastal air of Mumbai or the harsh UV index of the Indian interior, the coating maintains its finish and colour integrity over its warranted life. Available in a 10 year warranty for standard exterior applications and a 20 year warranty for premium façade specifications.

Typical applications Profile systems, aluminium façades, window and door frames, louvres, and outdoor lighting systems.

Epoxy Polyester (Hybrid)
Built for the
Interior
Interior & Semi-exposed

Polybond's Epoxy Polyester range is a high-performance hybrid coating designed for interior and semi-exposed applications. The hybrid chemistry combines the superior hardness and chemical resistance of epoxy with the colour stability and smooth flow of polyester, delivering a tough, attractive finish with excellent adhesion to steel and aluminium.

Not recommended for direct exterior exposure — prolonged UV contact will cause chalking and colour loss. Ideal for specifiers who need a decorative, durable indoor finish across a wide colour range and multiple gloss levels.

Typical applications Lighting fixture bodies and housings, electrical enclosures, industrial shelving, office furniture, metal cabinetry, and architectural interior metalwork.
